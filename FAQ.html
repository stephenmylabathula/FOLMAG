FAQ

What is FOL Magazine?

Fountain of Life (FoL) is an inspirational, interdenominational, apolitical (non-political) contemporary missions magazine published quarterly from, Visakhapatnam, AP., India. FoL includes articles, messages, thoughtful comments and relevant news written from a Christian perspective thereby bringing an awareness of missions and missionary endeavours. The articles are aimed at challenging the readers with the issues and happenings on the missionary frontiers and thus impact their spiritual lives to take up missions as a lifestyle.



Where is FOL Magazine circulated?

Fountain of Life (FoL) is a quarterly magazine circulated among Churches, Christian organisations, Bible colleges and university students, in India and overseas. Column viewpoints are of its respective authors, and do not necessarily represent the opinion of the FoL management or staff.



Can I write for FOL?

We welcome free-lance contribution of any article on missions and missionary work in India and abroad. Inspiring articles, first hand miracles experienced in challenging situations and other related articles that help Christian growth can be sent to us. However, the final decision to accept/reject depends on the editorial team. FoL endeavours to keep a good standard of articles and so kindly write to the Editor for Writer’s Guidelines before sending your articles. Fountain of Life is published 4 times in a year. Subscriptions are by a suggested donation. Advertising space is available for those targeting Christian community. For distributorship, subscriptions, membership and advertisements, write or call the Registered office in Visakhapatnam, India. Contents copyright ©2014 by Fountain of Life, India.
